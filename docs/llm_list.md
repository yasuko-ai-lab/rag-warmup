## GPT
A general-purpose LLM family known for strong reasoning, coding, and conversational ability. Widely used via ChatGPT and APIs.

## Claude
A family of LLMs focused on safety, alignment, and long-context understanding. Especially strong at document analysis and structured responses.

## Gemini
Multimodal LLMs capable of understanding text, images, audio, and video, designed for deep integration with everyday productivity tools.

## LLaMA
Open-weight LLMs designed for research and fine-tuning, popular for custom, on-prem, and open-source deployments.

## Grok
LLMs built for real-time reasoning and up-to-date information access, optimized for conversational and social-context use cases.

## Qwen
Multilingual LLMs with strong performance in Asian languages and enterprise-grade reasoning tasks.

## ERNIE
Knowledge-enhanced LLMs optimized for Chinese language understanding and enterprise applications.

## Mistral
Efficient, high-performance LLMs designed to deliver strong reasoning with smaller model sizes.

## Command
Enterprise-focused LLMs optimized for retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), search, and business workflows.

## Titan
General-purpose and embedding LLMs designed for scalable, cloud-native enterprise workloads.
